Why parents love it

💬

It starts conversations you wouldn't otherwise have

Most parent-child interaction is transactional — homework, dinner, bedtime. This question carves out space for something different. You'll be surprised what they say when you ask.

🧠

It teaches kids to reflect

Children who are asked to think about what mattered to them learn to recognize what matters. That's a skill that compounds for a lifetime.

🎙️

It captures how they saw the world at this age

The way a 4-year-old explains a great day is completely different from the way a 7-year-old does. Favorite Part preserves their voice, their logic, and their perspective — not just their face.

🔍

Every answer is searchable

Remember when she said something hilarious about the dog last spring? Find it. Every recording is transcribed and fully searchable.

Your videos never leave your device.

Favorite Part stores all video recordings locally on your phone — always. With your permission, some text data may be processed to enable optional app features. No ads. No data brokering. Your family's memories belong to your family.
